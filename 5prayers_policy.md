**Privacy Policy for 5 Prayers**

Effective date: 2026-03-08

Mishmash Labs ("we", "us", or "our") provides the 5 Prayers mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App uses, how that information is handled, and what choices you have.

This App is designed to work primarily on your device. We do not provide user accounts for the App, and we do not operate a backend service that stores your prayer settings or location history for this App.

**Summary**

- The App does not require account registration.
- The App does not include advertising SDKs.
- The App does not include analytics, crash reporting, or user-tracking SDKs.
- Most data used by the App is stored locally on your device.
- The App can use your location if you choose to grant permission.
- The App can generate PDF timetables and, if you choose, share those files with other apps or services.

**Information the App uses**

The App may access or store the following categories of information:

1. Location information

The App can use your device location to calculate prayer times for your area, display qibla information, and label your current location.

This may include:

- precise or approximate device coordinates, depending on what your device provides
- a derived place label such as city, region, or country
- derived qibla bearing and qibla distance values
- timezone information used for prayer timetable display

2. App settings and preferences stored on your device

The App stores settings locally on your device, including items such as:

- selected calculation method
- selected Asr method
- Hijri adjustment
- notification preferences and enabled prayer reminders
- notification reminder offset
- theme preference
- onboarding completion state
- your last selected or detected location details used by the App

3. Home screen widget data stored locally on your device

If you use the Android home screen widget, the App stores limited widget data locally on your device so the widget can display prayer times and the selected location label.

4. Files you choose to generate or share

If you use the monthly timetable export or share features, the App may create PDF files on your device. These files can include:

- the selected month
- prayer timetable values
- calculation and Asr method information
- Hijri adjustment
- the location name associated with the timetable

If you choose to share a generated PDF, the file and any text included with the share action are provided to the app or service you select.

**How the App uses information**

The App uses information only for the following purposes:

- calculating and displaying daily and monthly prayer times
- calculating and displaying qibla direction information
- letting you search for and select a manual location
- sending prayer reminder notifications if you enable them
- populating the Android home screen widget if you use it
- saving your preferences locally so the App remembers your settings
- generating and sharing timetable PDFs when you request those actions

**What we do not do**

We do not use the App to:

- sell your personal information
- create advertising profiles about you
- run in-app behavioral analytics
- collect account credentials for the App
- store your prayer settings on our own servers for this App

**Location and geocoding services**

When you use location features, the App relies on device and platform location capabilities and geocoding functionality provided by your operating system and installed libraries. For example:

- requesting your current location may use device location services
- converting coordinates to a place label may use platform geocoding services
- searching for a manual location may use geocoding or location lookup services available through your device platform

Those platform or service providers may process your request according to their own privacy terms. Mishmash Labs does not control those third-party privacy practices.

**Notifications**

If you enable prayer notifications, the App stores your notification preferences locally on your device and schedules reminder notifications on-device. On Android, the App requests notification permission before enabling reminders where required by the system.

**File exports and sharing**

When you create a monthly timetable PDF, the App writes the file to a device directory such as Downloads, Documents, temporary storage, or app storage depending on platform support. If you share a PDF, the selected receiving app or service will receive the file. You are responsible for deciding whether to share that content.

**Data sharing**

We do not share your information with third parties for advertising or analytics.

Information may still be handled by third parties in the following limited cases:

- your device operating system or platform services may process location or geocoding requests
- apps or services you choose when sharing a PDF will receive the shared file and related text
- app marketplaces or operating system vendors may receive technical information as part of installation, updates, billing, or platform operation under their own terms

**Data retention**

Most App data is retained locally on your device until you change it, clear the App's storage, uninstall the App, or delete exported files.

Generated PDFs remain where they were saved until you or your device removes them. Shared copies are controlled by the app or service you shared them to.

**Your choices**

You can:

- deny or revoke location access in your device settings
- deny or revoke notification access in your device settings
- choose manual location entry instead of current device location
- disable prayer notifications inside the App
- delete exported PDFs from your device
- uninstall the App to stop all further App-side data use on your device

Depending on your platform, you may also be able to clear the App's locally stored data through your device settings.

**Security**

We aim to keep the App limited to the data it needs and to store most data locally on your device. However, no device, platform, storage mechanism, or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

**Children's privacy**

The App is not directed specifically to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through a Mishmash Labs backend for this App.

If you believe a child has provided us personal information directly, contact us and we will review the situation.

**International use**

If platform or third-party services process location or geocoding requests, that processing may occur according to the infrastructure and policies of those providers, which may involve processing outside your country.

**Changes to this policy**

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date above. Your continued use of the App after an updated policy becomes effective means you acknowledge the updated policy.
