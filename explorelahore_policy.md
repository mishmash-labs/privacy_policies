# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Explore Lahore is a local-first city guide app. This policy explains what the app stores, what third-party services are used, and what happens when you open external links from the app.

## Information the app stores

Explore Lahore stores the following information on your device:

- Favorite places you save
- Itineraries you create
- Recently viewed places

This data stays on your device and is used only to support app features.

## Advertising

On Android, Explore Lahore uses Google Mobile Ads to show native advertisements in selected parts of the app. Google may collect and process device and advertising data under its own policies when an ad is requested or displayed.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## External links

Some places include links to Google Maps, websites, Instagram, or Facebook pages. When you open one of these links, you leave Explore Lahore and use the selected third-party service under that service's own terms and privacy policy.

## Analytics and accounts

Explore Lahore does not require account creation and does not currently include its own analytics, crash reporting, or user profile system.

## Children's privacy

Explore Lahore is a general audience travel and discovery app. It is not directed to children under 13.
